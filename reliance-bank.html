Reliance Bank: Mobile Banking App

Role: UX/UI Designer

Timeline: 2 weeks

Tools: Figma, Slack

Idea & Goal

As our FinTech sector continues to grow at a fast pace, they are faced with new problems that have surfaced over recent years. As technology continues to grow, mobile customer support needs to be of higher quality. In addition, the mobile needs of the GenZ and Millennial generations are not being met.

The goal is to create a native mobile banking app that will meet the most common needs of this demographic and build on the functionality based on customer feedback and research in future releases. The banking app is created with two different versions for iOS and Android using their guidelines.

High Level Demand

1

Customer can securely but easily log into the app

2

Customer can see all banking transactions and balances

3

Customer can pay bills using the app

4

Customers can deposit checks using the app

5

Customers can get notified of important actions on their account

High Fidelity Wireframes

Log Me In

The user can easily and securely log in according to their own preference: typing, face or finger scan, swipe pattern, or a token.

Sound the Alarms

The user will see alerts that need to be checked by the orange indicator on the profile picture.

Pay My Bills

Quickly pay your bills with the "Bills Pay" feature. Finding and adding a company is as easy as signing in.

Where's My Money?!

View recent transactions that are separated by incomes and expenses. Upon viewing all transactions, a visual graph separates the spendings into categories.

Money in the Bank

One feature for depositing a check allows the user to tap anywhere on the screen to take a photo. This inclusive features assists people who are missing a finger/thumb or cannot reach/tap certain areas on their screen.
